Website development is the process of creating, designing, building, and maintaining websites. It covers everything from the layout and design to coding, content creation, and performance optimization. Developers use programming languages like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to create responsive, user-friendly websites that provide seamless interaction across different devices. It also involves back-end development, which manages databases, server logic, and application integration. A good website must be accessible, fast, and secure. Website development is crucial for businesses looking to establish an online presence, build credibility, and interact effectively with customers around the world.

Digital marketing is the promotion of products or services through digital channels like search engines, social media, email, and websites to reach and engage potential customers. It enables businesses to reach a global audience, track performance in real-time, and target specific demographics more effectively. Compared to traditional marketing, it’s more cost-efficient and allows for personalized communication. In today’s online-first world, digital marketing helps brands stay visible, competitive, and connected to their audience 24/7.
